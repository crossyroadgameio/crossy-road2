Overview

Crossy Road is an endless arcade game developed by Hipster Whale. Inspired by the classic Frogger formula, the game challenges players to guide a character across roads, rivers, and train tracks while avoiding fast-moving cars, trucks, and other obstacles. The goal is simple: survive as long as possible and earn the highest score possible.

This fun and addictive game features easy-to-learn controls and vibrant voxel graphics, making it a perfect casual game for quick sessions or high-score chasing.

How to Play

Tap to move forward

Swipe left or right to avoid obstacles

Stay on the move or get caught by the eagle

Avoid cars, trucks, rivers, and trains

Survive as long as you can to achieve a high score

Features

Addictive gameplay with endless challenges

Vibrant voxel-style graphics

Unlockable characters that add personality to your run

Simple controls that anyone can pick up, but difficult to master
